Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti (or any pasta)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and melt butter.
  3. Add minced garlic and sautÃ© until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  4. Toss in the cooked pasta, add a splash of reserved pasta water, and mix well.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve hot with grated parmesan and fresh parsley.

Servings: 2

Prep time: 5 minutes

Cook time: 10 minutes
